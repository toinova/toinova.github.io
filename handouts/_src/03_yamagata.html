PROJECT 02 / YAMAGATA WEST NURSERY
Project 02 — Nursery school in a residential area

山形市西部保育所

Yamagata West Nursery
Program
保育園（定員 100 名）／こどもまんなか相談室
Site
山形県山形市・住宅地

山形県山形市の保育園の計画。100人の園児が生き生きと過ごすことができ、職員が気持ちよく働ける保育園となるよう計画しました。

安全・安心で過ごせる保育環境や、思い切り体を動かせる遊びの環境、インクルーシブ保育の提供機能、清潔な衛生環境を整備しています。

さらに「こどもまんなか相談室」として、発達相談や児童発達支援、民間保育施設等への支援、地域の子育て家庭への相談支援機能を持たせ、地域社会全体の子育てを支えます。

周辺への日照、音、渋滞防止、交通安全などに配慮しつつ、住宅地のスケールに馴染み、住民からも愛される保育園としました。

A nursery school project located in Yamagata City, Yamagata Prefecture. The facility is designed to provide a vibrant environment where 100 children can thrive and staff can work comfortably.

It is equipped with a safe and secure childcare environment, dynamic play areas for active physical movement, inclusive childcare capabilities, and a clean, hygienic environment.

It also features a “Child-Centered Consultation Room” that provides developmental support, assistance for private childcare facilities, and counseling services for local families, supporting child-rearing across the entire community.

While giving careful consideration to sunlight, noise mitigation, traffic congestion prevention, and traffic safety, the nursery school is designed to blend harmoniously with the scale of the residential neighborhood and become a beloved community asset.
